With current introduction to Tesla's trendy autopilot mode it makes this topic relevant to our current day, as having a 'safer' version of self driving cars increased the amount of people who have used this autopilot feature bringing this question of if it were to crash whose fault would it be to be more pertinent than ever. Importance

The topic of self-driving cars is an ever-important area with autonomous vehicles becoming closer and closer to being common on our roads. This raises critically important ethical questions surrounding what would happen were someone to be injured by a vehicle running on autopilot and who would be at fault. These questions must be resolved before autonomous cars become mainstream. For this reason, the topic of ethics surrounding autonomous vehicles is extremely important to research.
