Technology Choices

Self-driving cars have their benefits in the future as they continue to be developed such as improved efficiency and increase productivity on the road as they would be coded to maximise their efficiency especially among other self-driving cars. As the cars are further developed we will be able to see a reduction in traffic accidents. Although there would be in improvement in driving once we remove human error, the chances of a collision occurring is still there (Ryan, 2020, p. 1185-1208).

In the current day it seems that people despite knowing the fact that self-driving cars are more beneficial to society they seem to still be reluctant to want to ride or purchase cars that support the self-driving capability (Tan, Zhao, & Yang, 2022). Our current choices to make self driving cars more accepted is to improve their emotion design allowing people to feel less anxious while riding in these cars. This can be done by advertising the safety features allowing the customers to gain a different perspective on the self-driving cars (Tan, Zhao, & Yang, 2022). As for the future it is much harder to be able to avoid people losing their jobs if the implementation of self-driving cars becomes more prevalent.
