The Ethical Implications of Autonomous Vehicles

Technology has been advancing at a rapid rate in recent years , eventually leading to the reasearch and development of Self-Driving cars. These developments have also lead to questions concerning the safety of these cars, as well as the tougher questions regarding how the AI shoulc act in situations where a human could be put in danger as a consequence of the Self-Driving AI's decision.
